More About Me

Brief History

Having been at this stuff for a long time now, I attended the first Flash Forward in 2000 and I have battled Netscape 4. Through the years many things have changed and some things have stayed the same. What was for the web is now for almost everything. HTML, CSS, JavaScript to build websites, apps, IoT, digital signage, games, the list goes on…

I have used Flash for banners, games, online apps that act like HTML, hardware simulations, experiential museum installs and retail kiosks ( as Air ). HTML, CSS and JavaScript for your website, kiosk or app. Content management done in XML, JSON, ASP, PHP, Ruby, Python, JavaScript, the "Cloud".

The technology is not the determinant, the project goal and its best solution are. Today there are so many options that we can pick and choose, adapt and read the documentation as needed. Think hammer or screwdriver for that nail.

Short Bio

Shawn has been using technology to solve problems since 1997, including clients such as SpeedTV, Upper Deck, Coors, Heineken, Disney, Tupperware Brands, Motorola/Nextel, Bose, 3M, Harmonic, Riteaid and a wide variety of additional clients. His goal is to use the best technology for the solution at hand. His expertise stretches from websites and mobile or online applications, to proof of concept demos, to content managed multi-touch interactives. Shawn has created interactive installations for Tupperware Headquarters in Orlando Florida, 3M innovation Center in St. Paul Minnesota and the Witte Museum in San Antonio Texas as well as Motorola kiosks for printing tattoos and laser etching to mobile phones.

Shawn has a passion for learning and pushing the boundaries using the latest interactive technologies. In his free time, Shawn enjoys being a devoted husband since 2003 and father of two beautiful girls.
